Main features

🔔 Automatic Email Alerts

Real-time notifications

  • Automatic email reception when product recalls match your filters
  • Configuration of multiple email addresses (comma-separated)
  • Detailed emails with all recall information
  • Link to download official PDF notice
  • Complete information: reason, risks, actions to take, contact number

📊 Customizable Filters

Selection by categories and subcategories

  • Filtering by product categories (Food, Hygiene, etc.)
  • Filtering by subcategories (Meat, Dairy products, etc.)
  • Combination of multiple filters according to your needs
  • Option to follow all recalls (no filter)
  • Flexible configuration according to your activity

🖥️ Modern and Intuitive Interface

Optimal user experience

  • Instant search with real-time filtering
  • Card display with product images
  • Smooth navigation with pagination
  • Complete details of each recall in a dedicated window
  • Dedicated tab in product sheets for direct consultation
  • Direct download of official PDF documents

📦 Integration with Products

Dedicated tab in product sheets

  • Direct consultation of recalls linked to each product
  • Automatic display of recalls matching the product reference
  • Detailed information: reason, risks, publication date
  • Contact number for more information
  • Visual alerts for concerned products

📈 Homepage Widget

Visual tracking of latest recalls

  • Widget displaying the latest product recalls
  • Automatic update when new recalls appear
  • Quick access to important information
  • Immediate visibility of alerts

⚙️ Automatic Import

Synchronization with RappelConso

  • Scheduled job for automatic recall import
  • Initial import of all available recalls
  • Automatic update every 10 minutes
  • Synchronization with official RappelConso website
  • Local database for quick consultation

⚠️ Important information

Data source: RappelConso (France)

This module uses data from the official RappelConso website, managed by the Directorate General for Competition, Consumer Affairs and Fraud Control (DGCCRF). The tracked recalls mainly concern products marketed in France. For other countries, please check data availability.

Prerequisites

  • Dolibarr ERP & CRM installed (version 18.0 or higher)
  • AploseFramework module enabled (free, available on DoliStore) - Required
  • Products module enabled
  • Email configuration in Dolibarr to receive alerts
  • Activation of scheduled job "Product Recall Verification"

Use cases

  • Distributors: Track recalls of products you sell
  • Restaurateurs: Be alerted of food recalls concerning your suppliers
  • Retailers: Monitor recalls of products in your catalog
  • Importers: Track recalls of imported products
  • Quality managers: Centralize product recall tracking
  • Compliance: Comply with obligations to withdraw recalled products
